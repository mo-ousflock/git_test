Ingredients to make a good curry bread :

  • Grass
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Fart sauce
  • Dog toe nails

Steps to make a good curry bread

  1. Put 27 blades of grass in a plastic cup
  2. Fart in a bag of water to make fart sauce and add to cup
  3. Add 3 dog toe nails into the cup as a thickening agent
  4. Add to pot to with 37 potspoons of curry powder an 98 kilograms of garlic powder
